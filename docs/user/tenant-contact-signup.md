# Contact and email list forms

Artist contact pages include a contact form and an email-list signup form. When visitors submit either form, ArtsFolio shows a sending indicator and then displays the result on the same page.

The “I’m human” checkbox may be disabled briefly after the page loads. Wait a moment, check the box, then submit. This is ArtsFolio’s built-in spam protection and does not use Google reCAPTCHA.

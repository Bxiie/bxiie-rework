# Billing, checkout, and footer links

Tenant owners can review plan usage from **Admin → Billing**. The page shows the current plan, included limits, current usage, online checkout availability, and any complementary billing status.

If online checkout is enabled for the plan, shoppers can add available works to a cart and complete checkout. ArtsFolio may send cart reminders after 12 and 24 hours when a shopper entered an email address but did not finish checkout.

Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn links entered in site content settings appear in the footer of public tenant pages.

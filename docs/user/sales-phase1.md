# Buying artwork

Artwork marked for sale shows a price on the portfolio and artwork detail pages.

On the artwork detail page, use **Contact the artist about this artwork** to ask about purchase, shipping, pickup, installation, edition details, or availability.

Some works are one-off original pieces. Others may be multiples such as prints, postcards, shirts, or editioned objects.

Online checkout is being added through Stripe in a later sales phase.

# Onboarding

Click **Onboarding** in your admin sidebar whenever you need help setting up or
reviewing your site.

From that tab you can open:

- your setup checklist;
- the guided admin tour;
- the admin function index;
- training resources.

You can also reset onboarding progress. The reset does not remove or alter your
site content, artwork, settings, users, or sales.

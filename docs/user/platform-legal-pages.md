# ArtsFolio Terms and Privacy

ArtsFolio publishes platform legal information at:

- Terms: `https://artsfol.io/terms`
- Privacy: `https://artsfol.io/privacy`

The Terms explain account use, tenant sites, public artwork/content, directory opt-in, sales, plans, fees, acceptable use, and platform rights.

The Privacy Policy explains collected data, OAuth login data, public tenant content, contact forms, email lists, sales records, cookies, security, retention, and data deletion requests.

# Signing in with Google or Facebook

ArtsFolio supports signing in with Google or Facebook from the login and signup pages.

## How to sign in

1. Open the ArtsFolio login page.
2. Choose `Continue with Google` or `Continue with Facebook`.
3. Approve the login with the provider.
4. ArtsFolio signs you in and sends you to your admin area, platform admin area, or signup page depending on your account.

## Notes

- ArtsFolio needs your provider email address to create or find your account.
- If Facebook does not share an email address, use Google or email/password login instead.
- If you already have an ArtsFolio account with the same email, the social login is linked to that account.
- You can still use email/password login when it is configured for your account.

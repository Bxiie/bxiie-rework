# Managing a large artwork collection

Use the filters at the top of the Artworks page to narrow a large collection by publication status, sale status, image availability, section, or search text. Fifty artworks are shown at a time.

The Artwork Placement Matrix is also paginated. Save each page after changing home-page or section assignments. Saving one page does not alter artworks on other pages.

Visitors see the public portfolio in pages of 24 artworks.

## Choosing how many artworks to display

Use the **Artworks per page** dropdown on the portfolio, Artworks administration page, or Artwork Placement Matrix. Admin pages default to 50 artworks and allow 10 through 100 in increments of 10. The public portfolio defaults to 24 while also allowing the standard 10-through-100 choices.

## Moving one page at a time

Artwork aggregation pages include **− Previous** and **Next +** buttons around the numbered page links. Your selected section, filters, and artworks-per-page choice remain active as you move backward or forward.

## Portfolio paging

Portfolio paging changes only the artwork area rather than reloading the entire site page. Use **‹ Previous**, **Next ›**, page numbers, section choices, or the artworks-per-page selector. The address bar still changes, allowing a filtered or paged portfolio view to be bookmarked. Without JavaScript, the same controls continue to work with normal page navigation.

# Curation workflow

Tenant owners invite people as **Tenant admin**, **Editor**, or **User**. Users can add artwork to the central or editor-specific curation queue from artwork cards and include a note. Editors review their queue plus the central queue, publish or decline artwork, and reply. Replies appear on the submitting user’s `/messages` page. The workflow is available on paid plans only.

Unpublished artwork and empty advance-planning portfolio sections remain hidden from visitors. Any logged-in tenant member can see them with an **Unpublished** marker.

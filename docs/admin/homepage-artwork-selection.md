# Selecting artworks for the home page

The **Home Page** entry on **Portfolio Sections** is a special section.

1. Click **Portfolio Sections** in the tenant-admin sidebar.
2. Click **Manage Home Page artworks**.
3. Select portfolio artworks. Draft artwork may be selected; whether it appears publicly follows the normal draft behavior used by the portfolio.
4. Set ordering numbers. Lower numbers appear first.
5. Click **Save Home Page artworks**.

Home Page cannot be renamed, archived, or deleted. Site-type branding assets do
not appear in its artwork picker.

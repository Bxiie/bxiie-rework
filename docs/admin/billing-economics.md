# Billing economics administration

Use Platform Admin → Pricing to set each plan's monthly price, feature limits, sales availability, credit card percentage fee, and fixed credit card charge. Use the Platform sales commission field for the ArtsFolio commission applied to platform-processed sales.

Complementary tenants do not pay subscription fees. They still pay the configured platform commission and credit card charges on sales.

Tenant billing screens disclose platform commission, credit card charges, and estimated proceeds for example sales so artists understand expected payout before selling work.

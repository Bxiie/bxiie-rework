# Recording checklist

## Before recording
- Use the Northstar Studio training tenant.
- Confirm all navigation pages load.
- Clear notices and temporary errors.
- Use demonstration data only.
- Close personal tabs and apps.
- Disable desktop notifications.
- Set browser window to 1920 × 1080.
- Confirm microphone level without clipping.
- Keep the cursor visible.
- Prepare the public site in a second tab.
- Do not display credentials during sign-in.

## During recording
- Speak at approximately 135–145 words per minute.
- Leave half a second after each click.
- Keep the sidebar visible during navigation explanations.
- Avoid rapidly circling controls with the cursor.
- Do not open customer records, real messages, refunds, or account secrets.
- Record each chapter as a separate take when possible.

## After recording
- Remove pauses longer than two seconds.
- Mask any accidental personal information.
- Normalize audio.
- Add captions from the supplied SRT.
- Add the six on-screen text cards.
- Confirm chapter timestamps after the final edit.
- Test playback at 1080p and 720p.
- Check captions on phone width.
- Hold the ending card for at least three seconds.
